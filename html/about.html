About US

Ethio-Golden Ethiopia Tours PLC is locally owned tour Operation Company based in Axum, Tigray. Ethiopia established by skilled, certified and experienced tourism professionals. Our long years experience in the tourism industry makes us one of most successful and reputed tour operation company in Ethiopia in terms of professionalism and quality services. We know how much enjoyable travelling can be when someone you trust looks out for your specific interests, we are your ideal local travel representative offer a gateway for finding the best travel deals for Ethiopia. We provide you with an extensive and wide range of tour packages, travel arrangements destined to Ethiopia. Among our specialties sightseeing tours to the famous Axum and Omo valley cultural tours, Addis Ababa city tour with short and weekend excursions in the surroundings, Danakil Depression adventure tours, Trekking tours to Simien and Bale Mountains national parks, bird watching tours, special interest customized tour, photo safari, flight and hotel reservations, car and equipment rentals, transportation facilities and arrangements. Our qualified, efficient and cost effective tour packages designed to provide our highly esteemed customer with the ultimate guaranteed service experience with care, comfort, courtesy, safety and life time unforgettable memories. Above all our customer’s utmost enjoyment and satisfaction is our priority.
